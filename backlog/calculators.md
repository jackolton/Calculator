## Validation hypotheses (revisit monthly)

- Are Tier 1 calcs ranking top 20 within 60 days of indexing? If not, diagnose: content thin, backlinks absent, or wrong keyword match?
- Is one calc doing >50% of traffic? If yes, double down — build the next 5 calcs adjacent to that winner.
- Is DSCR calc monetizing? DSCR lead payouts are the primary revenue thesis. If CTR to lender is under 2%, redesign the CTA.

## Notes on search volume estimates

All MSV numbers above are hypotheses, not measured. Validate via Keyword Planner, Ahrefs free trial, or Semrush before committing heavy effort to any single calc. The numbers are useful as relative priorities even if absolute values are off.
